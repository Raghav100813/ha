6.CLEAN WATER & SANITATION

To ensure access to safe water and sanitation for all.

Sanitation


The department is effectively implementing the Swacch Bharat Mission (SBM) Gramin intending to provide sustainable sanitation to every rural household in Karnataka. Rural Karnataka was declared as Open defecation-free on 19th November 2018. Regular awareness programs are being organized in Rural Karnataka to achieve sustainable sanitation. Currently, SBM (G) Phase-2 is in progress, it aims for sustainable sanitation and implementation of ODF+.

Drinking Water


With an aim to provide 55litres per capita per day to every household in Rural Karnataka through Functional Household Tap Connections (FHTCs), Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) has been implemented. Many drinking water programmes were also introduced earlier, currently Mane Manege Gange (JJM Karnataka) is being implemented passionately.

Sustainable Developement Goals


Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that all people enjoy peace and prosperity. There are 17 SDGs in total, ranging from no poverty and zero hunger to quality education and gender equality, among others. They were adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015 as part of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.
