           Challenges
        </h2>
        <p>
         Challenges like inadequate infrastructure, pollution, water scarcity, and funding shortages hinder universal access to safe water and sanitation. Marginalized communities and developing regions are especially affected, impeding progress toward this crucial goal.
        </p>
