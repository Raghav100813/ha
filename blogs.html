Importance

Clean Water and Sanitation, is essential for health, hygiene, and sustainable development. It provides safe drinking water, sanitation, reduces disease, and fosters economic growth and environmental conservation, especially in underserved areas and among marginalized populations.

Read More

Challenges

Ensuring clean water and sanitation faces various challenges worldwide. Some include inadequate infrastructure, water scarcity, pollution, lack of funding, and unequal access, especially in rural areas and developing countries. Climate change exacerbates these issues, affecting water availability and quality.
